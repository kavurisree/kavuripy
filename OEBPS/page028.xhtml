As I stretched,
a sweet smell filled the air.
All around me,
colorful flowers were in full bloom.
I looked at the fairy house.
The honeysuckle and berries
were gone.
“The fairies came!” I cried.
“They came,
but I missed the whole thing.”
Peter started to sniffle.
“Don’t cry. Look!” I said.
